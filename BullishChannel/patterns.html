Chart Patterns

Cup & Handle

Strength: Bullish

Shape: U-shaped formation (CUP) of the candles followed by a channel movement (HANDLE) in the candles

Description: Cup and Handles reveals the shifts in supply and demand of a stock tickerâ€“ from excess supplies overwhelming the demands to demands overwhelming the supplies.

Signal: A break above the channel indicates a buy signal

Double Top

Strength: Bearish

Shape: A linear constant resistance point where price gets rejected twice

Description: Great selling pressure at the resistance point led by further drop in price

Signal: A sell is indicated once after the second rejection of the resistance point and breaking the support area

Double Bottom

Strength: Bullish

Shape: A linear constant support point where price bounces off twice

Description: Great buying pressure at the support point led by further rises in price

Signal: A buy is indicated once after the second bounce off of the support point and breaking the resistance area

Bull Flag

Strength: Bullish

Shape: A sharp rise in candle(s) (POLE) followed by a small consolidation period (FLAG)

Description: Bullish Flag Formation reveals a surge of demands, outweighing the supplies, followed by a period in which supplies and demands are almost at a equilibrium.

Signal: A break above the FLAG indicates a buy signal

Bear Flag

Strength: Bear

Shape: A sharp decline in candle(s) (POLE) followed by a small consolidation period (FLAG)

Description: Bearish Flag Formation reveals a surge of supplies, outweighing the demands, followed by a period in which supplies and demands are almost at a equilibrium.

Signal: A break below the FLAG indicates a sell signal

Head & Shoulders Pattern

Strength: Bearish

Shape: Price in an uptrend:
1) gets rejected by the first major layer of resistance
2) Bouncing off support area, breaks above the first resistance (SHOULDER). Price gets rejected off of the second resistance point (HEAD).
3) Price falling back and again bouncing off of support level, retest the first resistance point (SHOULDER) and fails to break. Price falls back to support level and breaks support to start a downtrend

Description: Reversal pattern in which price in an uptrend with support zone fails to break through the layers of resistance. Ultimately, the price falls below the support area and continues to descend

Signal: Sell signal is generated after the last break of the support area

Inverse Head & Shoulders Pattern

Strength: Bullish

Shape: Price in an downtrend:
1) gets bounces off the first major layer of support
2) Rejected from the resistance mark, breaks below the first support (INVERSE SHOULDER). Price bounces off of the second support point (INVERSE HEAD).
3) Price falling back and again rejected by the resistance level, retest the first support point (INVERSE SHOULDER) and fails to break. Price falls back to resistance level and breaks resistance to start a uptrend

Description: Reversal pattern in which price in an downtrend with resistance zone fails to break through the layers of support. Ultimately, the price rises above the resistance area and continues to ascend

Signal: Sell signal is generated after the last break of the support area

Pennant

Strength: Bullish and Bearish

Shape: Support and Resistance level converges at the same rate

Description: Decreasing resistance level showing increasing selling pressure with increasing resistance level showing increasing buying pressure. These two will eventually converge.

Signal: A break above the pennant indicates a buy signal. A break below the pennant indicates a sell signal.

Rectangle

Strength: Bullish & Bearish

Shape: Side-way channel (RECTANGLE) of the candles

Description: Rectangle reveals the equilibrium of supplies and demand as one is not able to exceed another for a period of time.

Signal: A break above the rectangle indicates a buy signal. A break below the rectangle indicates a sell signal.

Ascending Triangle

Strength: Bullish

Shape: Candles form a solid base at the top as resistance, while forming a linear increasing support level.

Price pattern of candles rejecting a solid resistance zone shows a solid level of supplies overwhelmingthe demands, while the increasing support level shows the increasing demands over supplies.

Signal: When the lines of support and resistance converges, it indicates a buy signal

Descending Triangle

Strength: Bearish

Shape: Candles form a solid base at the bottom as support, while forming a linear decreasing resistance level.

Price pattern of candles rejecting a solid support zone shows a solid level of demands overwhelmingthe supplies, while the decreasing support level shows the increasing supplies over demands.

Signal: When the lines of support and resistance converges, it indicates a sell signal

Triple Top

Strength: Bearish

Shape: A linear constant resistance point where price gets rejected three times

Description: Immense selling pressure at the resistance point led by further drop in price

Signal: A sell is indicated once after the third rejection of the resistance point and breaking the support area

Triple Bottom

Strength: Bullish

Shape: A linear constant support point where price bounces three times

Description: Immense buying pressure at the support mark led by further rise in price

Signal: A buy is indicated once after the third bounce off of the support point and breaking the resistance area

Wedge

Strength: Bullish & Bearish

Shape: Support and Resistance level converges at a different rate

Description: Decreasing resistance level showing increasing selling pressure with increasing resistance level showing increasing buying pressure. These two will eventually converge and whichever one has the greater slope will determine the outlook.

Signal: A break above the wedge indicates a buy signal. A break below the wedge indicates a sell signal.
